Filipino Desserts

1. Halo-halo 


A signature dessert of the Philippines, halo-halo features layers of condensed milk, shaved ice, sweetened beans, fruit, and much more. The coldness and the flavorful taste of the Halo-halo is perfect to refresh and cool down. As such in the tropical country such as Philippines it is a perfect dessert to combat the heat. And by reading the recipe down below you can learn this cold, refreshing, and tasteful Halo-halo.


By Daniel Rojas  |   November 16, 2022

Photo by Kuya Jay



Ingredients

  • Shaved ice: A crucial ingredient for both its cold temperature and its crunchy texture.
  • Evaporated milk: This ingredient adds both sweetness and a rich, creamy consistency.
  • Plantains (or bananas): Halo-halo often includes saba plantains cooked in sugar syrup to both soften and sweeten them.
  • Ube (or yams): A layer of cubed purple yams is common in halo-halo, and some versions also include ube jam and a scoop of ube ice cream on top
  • Beans: This ingredient might surprise those who aren't used to halo-halo, but sweetened beans (either red beans or mung beans) lend a welcome chewy texture.
  • Coconut: Some recipes utilize shaved coconut, others contain coconut gelatin, but most feature some coconut element.
  • Sugar palm fruit and/or jackfruit: These fruits grow abundantly throughout the Philippines, so it stands to reason that they constitute a layer of halo-halo.
  • Flan: Not all versions of halo-halo use flan as an ingredient, but some include both this custardy dessert and a scoop of ube ice cream as toppings.


Procedures

  1. Step1
    Place jack fruit in a bowl. Add white beans and red beans and mix thoroughly.
  2. Step2
    Place 2 tablespoons of the jackfruit mixture in a tall glass. Fill glass 2/3-full of shaved ice. Pour evaporated milk over the shaved ice. Mix using a teaspoon. Top with 2 tablespoons vanilla ice cream. Repeat layering the halo halo in 3 more glasses.




2. Chocolate Nutella Pandesal  


Pandesal is the most famous bread in the Philippines. The bread is known for its blandness which is perfect to partner with different kind of spread or beverages. As such today we will partner pandesal with nutella (a chocolate spread). This would surely flavor up our pandesal and bring a wonderful taste that match perfectly with pandesal.


By Daniel Rojas  |   November 16, 2022

Photo by Dave Cook


PrepTime  CookTime

1 hour 30 mins   15 mins

Total Time:  Servings

1 hrs 45 mins    8


Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup warm water, 105 F to 115 F
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 2 ounces unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 4 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 4 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil, for kneading
  • 1 cup Nutella, well chilled
  • 1/4 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup graham cracker crumbs


Procedures

  1. Step1
    In the bowl of a stand mixer, combine yeast, warm water, and 1 tablespoon of the sugar. Let the mixture stand for 5 minutes or until foamy.
  2. Step2
    Add the milk, butter, eggs, the remaining sugar, salt, and cocoa powder. Stir everything to combine.
  3. Step3
    Using the dough hook, add 2 and 1/4 cups of flour and mix on low speed until the dry ingredients are moistened. Increase the speed and continue to mix until the ingredients are fully incorporated.
  4. Step4
    With the mixer running, add 2 cups of flour gradually. Keep mixing, occasionally scraping the bottom and sides of the mixer bowl, until the dough starts to gather in the center of the bowl.
  5. Step5
    If the dough is still too wet and sticky, add the remaining 1/2 cup of flour and keep mixing until the dough has lost a lot of stickiness
  6. Step6
    Lightly grease a clean working surface and your hands with cooking oil. Turn the dough over on the working surface and knead until it is smooth and elastic.
  7. Step7
    Place the kneaded dough in a lightly oiled bowl and cover with a plastic film or a clean towel. Let rise for about 1 1/2 to 2 hours or until doubled in size.
  8. Step8
    When the dough has doubled in size, gently deflate, and turn out on a work surface. Form into a log and divide into 20 pieces.
  9. Step9
    Take one portion, roll into a ball, and then flatten. Spoon about a tablespoon of Nutella in the center and fold the edges of the dough around the center to cover the filling.
  10. Step10
    In a shallow plate, combine breadcrumbs and graham cracker crumbs.
  11. Step11
    Shape it into a smooth ball and press the ends underneath to seal. Roll the dough in the bread and graham crumbs mixture. Repeat with the rest of the dough portions.
  12. Step12
    Arrange the assembled chocolate pandesal on a parchment-lined baking pan and cover loosely with a clean kitchen towel. Let rise for another hour or until puffy.
  13. Step13
    14. Bake in a preheated 350 F for about 18 to 20 minutes. The tops should feel firm to touch with a bit of bounce.




3. Puto  


Puto is one of the most famous kakanin here in the Philippines. The fluffy, soft and cheesy puto is a dessert that you wouldn't like to miss out. As such come and join me in cooking this cute and tasteful Puto.


By Daniel Rojas  |   November 16, 2022

Photo by Lutong Bahay


PrepTime  CookTime

 5mins     30 mins

Total Time: Servings

 35 mins    3


Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated white sugar
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 cup fresh milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 5 cups water for steaming


Procedures

  1. Step1
    Pour the water into a steamer or cooking pot. Let boil.
  2. Step2
    Sift flour, sugar, and baking powder. Combine in a bowl. Mix we
  3. Step3
    Gradually add the milk while stirring. Continue to stir until the texture of the mixture becomes smooth.
  4. Step4
    Stir-in the vanilla extract and half of the cheese.
  5. Step5
    Pour the mixture into small to medium sized molds. Arrange in a steamer. Cover and steam for 25 minutes. Top each puto with the remaining cheese. Steam for 5 minutes more.
  6. Step6
    Take the steamed cheese puto out of the steamer. Let it cool down.
  7. Step7
    Gently remove the puto from the mold with the help of a spatula.
  8. Step8
    Arrange in serving plate. Serve.
  9. Step9
    Share and enjoy!
